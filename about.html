About admin.
Why play?
I started in tibia in 2007, I believe it was version 7.9 at the time, in the world of Amera. Land of ancient legends like Eternal Oblivion and Will Hunting.
Since then I have had social experiences within the game that I believe should be lived by more people, both experiences that resulted in some success, and those that resulted in failure or some loss.
As my profession and career were built by the will and curiosity that Tibia and the bots gave me at the time. And I would like to create that same enthusiasm for the new generations to come.
The intention of this server is NOT to make you put your life aside as you did in adolescence, to live inside a retro game and earning profits for the owner.
THERE ARE NO PAYMENT METHODS IMPLEMENTED AND THERE IS NO ADVANTAGE THROUGH PAYMENT.
The server is constantly being updated and is a project that had the initial idea in 2019 and was shelved for a while, but is now getting off the ground.
I hope you understand that I don't have support from other people and I do all the systems and fixes myself. So be patient.
I also emphasize that if you are interested in chatting, criticizing, talking about the server or anything else, you can find me by email writing in English or Portuguese: tibiacconta1@gmail.com
